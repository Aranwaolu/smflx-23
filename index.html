What is Single, Married, and Family Life Xperience

Singles, Married, and Family Life Xperience is an acronym for SMFLX.

It is spiritual robust engagement platform birthed by the inspiration of the Spirit of God through his servant, Pastor Thompson Ehima to facilitate the follows

  • Preparing single, married and family that are willing and ready to engage the purpose and plan of God unto the end.
  • Downloading prerequisite insight, skill and strength of the knowledge and wisdom of God to combat invisible threats to the prosperity of the purpose and plan for creation.
  • Raising the banner and standard of the truth in an increasing dark and gloom generations of single, married and family.
  • Giving divine direction and guidance on how to navigate different phases, stages and seasons of single, married and family life
  • Accessing and discerning prophetic courses playing out in our relationships as single, married and family lest we miss out God ordination.

The lead conveners are Pastor Thompson Ehima and Pastor (Mrs.) Modupeola Thompson-Ehima, whose intense passion for all is to bring clarity and stability in fulfilling God’s original purpose and plan for the living. Join us for SMFLX 2023 at this year Eid al-Adha holiday season.

THE WAKE OF A NEW DAWN

As humans, we lack wholesomeness and balance that commends us as complete in the sight of God. We’ve all been fed and configured in pattern and definition of what life is. This has made us fill up the hole we carry around with a limitless number of things we desire and think are call theme our life. The agonies and pains we borne had made and driven away independence from family, our past experiences and mistakes have fashioned our decisions and choices. And now, we are a shadow of the being God conceived and intended in His mind when He created and formed us in our mothers’ womb.

Amidst growing chaos, confusion and challenge in the world we live in today, the church cannot play victim nor beaten down by overwhelming evil.

There is increasing desire in the Body of Christ for the supernatural awakening, great revival and outpouring of the Spirit in greater measures in these last times. But we lack clear direction and instruction on how to harness the mind of the Spirit and cultivate the victorious life of the same Spirit of Jesus Christ.

SMFLX promises to break forth newness of life and spirit; open up fresh founts within you and a new lease of power to address various issues plaguing this generation.

So we ask, do you desire life? Do you want to be whole as God is? This conference is a must to every individual and family; live life in fullness.
