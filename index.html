What is Single, Married, and Family Life Xperience (SMFLX)

SMFLX is an acronym for Single, Married, and Family Life Xperience.

It is a spiritually-robust platform birthed by the inspiration of the Spirit of God through His servant, Pastor Thompson Ehima to facilitate the following:

  • Preparing singles, married, and families willing and ready to engage the purpose and plan of God unto the end.
  • Downloading prerequisite insight, skill, and strength of the knowledge and wisdom of God to combat invisible threats to the prosperity of the purpose and plan of God for creation.
  • Raising the banner and standard of the truth in an increasingly dark and gloomy generation of singles, married, and families.
  • Giving divine direction and guidance on how to navigate different phases, stages, and seasons of life as a single, married, and family.
  • Accessing and discerning prophetic courses playing out in our relationships as single, married, and family, lest we miss out on God's ordination.

The lead conveners are Pastor Thompson Ehima and Pastor (Mrs.) Modupeola Thompson-Ehima, whose intense passion for all is to bring clarity and stability in fulfilling God’s original purpose and plan for the living.

THE WAKE OF A NEW DAWN

As humans, we lack the wholesomeness and balance that commends us as complete in the sight of God. We’ve all been fed a false pattern and configured with a negative definition of what life is. This has made us fill up the hole we carry around with a limitless number of things we desire, think, and have themed as our life. The agonies and pains we have borne have made us stone-like in our relationships and driven away dependence on family; our past experiences and mistakes have fashioned our decisions and choices. And now, we are a shadow of the being God conceived and intended in His mind when He created and formed us in our mothers’ wombs.

Amidst the growing chaos, confusion, and challenge in the world we live in today, the church cannot play victim nor be beaten down by overwhelming evil.

There is an increasing desire in the Body of Christ for a supernatural awakening, great revival, and outpouring of the Spirit in greater measures for these last times. But we lack clear direction and instruction on how to harness the mind of the Spirit and cultivate the victorious life of the same Spirit of Jesus Christ.

SMFLX promises to break forth newness of life and spirit, open up fresh founts within you, and a new lease of power to address various issues plaguing this generation.

So we ask, do you desire life? Do you want to be whole as God is?  This conference is a must for every individual and family; to live life in its fullness.
